Biography

I am now a final year Ph.D. student at The University of Adelaide. I received my M.Eng degree from East China Normal University in Dec, 2019. My current interests lie in domain generalization, deepfake detection, and low-level vision tasks. If you are interested in my work, please do not hesitate to contact.
